# High Risk Pregnancy Guidelines (PregaCare)

## URGENT NOTICE
Your score indicates a High-Risk profile. This requires immediate medical intervention. These guidelines are for information only and NOT a substitute for a hospital visit.

## Immediate Steps
1. **Consult a Specialist**: Visit an Obstetrician/Gynecologist immediately.
2. **Bed Rest**: Absolute bed rest is often required to reduce strain on the heart and uterus.
3. **Emergency Signs**: If you experience severe headache, blurred vision, sudden swelling of face/hands, or vaginal bleeding, go to the Emergency Room immediately.

## Diagnostic Needs
- Request a full Bio-physical Profile (BPP) ultrasound.
- Ensure regular Non-Stress Tests (NST) to monitor the baby's heart rate.

## Regional Action
- If your District Safety Score is low, consider relocating temporarily to a Tier-1 city (like Delhi, Mumbai, or Chandigarh) with advanced Neonatal ICU (NICU) facilities.
