# Low Risk Pregnancy Guidelines (PregaCare)

## Overview
Your assessment indicates a healthy pregnancy with low clinical risk and high regional safety. The focus here is on maintenance and wellness.

## Nutritional Guidelines
- **Iron & Folic Acid**: Essential for baby's brain development. Continue supplements as prescribed.
- **Hydration**: Drink 8-10 glasses of water daily.
- **Whole Foods**: Focus on leafy greens, lentils, and dairy.

## Activity
- **Walking**: 30 minutes of brisk walking is highly recommended.
- **Yoga**: Prenatal yoga can help with flexibility and stress management.

## Monitoring
- Monitor fetal movement (kick counts) daily in the third trimester.
- Standard monthly checkups are usually sufficient unless new symptoms arise.
