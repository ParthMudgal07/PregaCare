# Mid Risk Pregnancy Guidelines (PregaCare)

## Overview
A Mid-Risk score suggests that while there is no immediate emergency, your vitals or location require extra vigilance. 

## Clinical Precautions
- **Blood Pressure**: Monitor twice daily (morning and evening). Contact your doctor if Systolic > 140 or Diastolic > 90.
- **Blood Sugar**: Maintain a low-glycemic diet. Avoid white sugar, white bread, and excessive potatoes.
- **Weight Gain**: Track weekly weight; sudden spikes can indicate fluid retention (edema).

## Lifestyle Adjustments
- **Salt Reduction**: Reduce salt to prevent swelling and BP spikes.
- **Rest**: Ensure at least 2 hours of rest during the day in addition to night sleep.

## Emergency Planning
- Keep a "Go-Bag" ready with your medical records.
- Identify the nearest hospital with a 24/7 maternity ward.
